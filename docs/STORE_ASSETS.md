# Store-asset checklist (B3)

What the two stores need **beyond** the app icon/splash the build already carries. These are
**captured or designed at submission** — the logo file alone doesn't cover them. Nothing here
is marketing copy or fake screenshots to invent; it's the list Adam fills from the running app.

Sizes are current as of the 2025 store requirements — re-confirm in App Store Connect / Play
Console at submission, as Apple/Google revise device classes.

## Apple App Store

- **Marketing app icon** — 1024×1024 PNG, **flattened/opaque, no alpha, no rounded corners**
  (Apple rounds it). This is the same square the in-app icon derives from; upload it in App
  Store Connect. (The build's icon is generated by EAS; this store copy is separate.)
- **Screenshots** — captured from the running app, portrait. Up to 10 per device class.
  - **6.9" iPhone** (16 Pro Max class): **1320×2868** — required.
  - **6.5" iPhone** (11 Pro Max / XS Max): **1242×2688** — required if 6.9" not supplied for all;
    simplest is to provide both.
  - **iPad** (if `ios.supportsTablet` is true): 13" **2064×2752** / 12.9" **2048×2732**. The app is
    a portrait phone app — decide whether to set **`ios.supportsTablet: false`** to avoid owing
    iPad screenshots (recommended unless iPad is a target).
- **App preview video** — optional; skip for launch.
- **Text/metadata** — name, subtitle, promotional text, description, keywords, support URL,
  marketing URL (optional), **privacy policy URL** (required).
- **Privacy nutrition labels** — declare data collected (see "Data declarations" below).
- **Age rating** questionnaire.

## Google Play

- **Store listing icon** — **512×512 PNG, 32-bit with alpha**. (Distinct from the in-app
  adaptive icon; Play shows this in the listing.)
- **Feature graphic** — **1024×500 PNG/JPEG, no alpha** — **required**. This is a designed
  banner (badge + wordmark/tagline on navy `#06122f`), **not** just the logo dropped in — needs
  layout. Flag for the designer or compose from the badge + a short tagline.
- **Phone screenshots** — **min 2, up to 8**; portrait e.g. **1080×1920**; each side 320–3840px.
- **Tablet screenshots** — 7" and 10" **only if** you target tablets (mirror the iPad decision).
- **Text/metadata** — short description (≤80 chars), full description (≤4000 chars),
  **privacy policy URL** (required).
- **Data safety form** — declare data collected (below).
- **Content rating** (IARC) questionnaire.

## Data declarations (both stores) — what this app actually collects

Fill the privacy forms honestly from the real data flows:
- **Email** (auth), **phone** (optional, at signup), **name/gender/level** (profile).
- **Photos** — the credit-request **screenshot upload** (payment proof).
- **Push token** — `device_push_tokens` for notifications.
- **Payment**: handled **out of band** (InstaPay/cash reported by the player); Paymob is
  mothballed behind a flag. No card data touches the app today.
- A **privacy policy URL** is required by both stores and is currently **missing** — needs
  writing/hosting before submission.

## Not covered by the logo (summary for Adam)

2. **Play feature graphic** (1024×500) — a designed banner.
3. **Store icons** at each store's spec (Apple 1024 opaque, Play 512 w/ alpha).
4. **Privacy policy URL** — write + host.
5. **Descriptions / keywords** — real copy, not fabricated here.
